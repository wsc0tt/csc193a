Visit Santa Cruz County, California

Santa Cruz is one of my favorite places to spend the weekend in the state of California. Santa Cruz is located on the coast of the Pacific Ocean, nestled in between the Santa Cruz Mountains and Monterey Bay, making it the perfect weekend escape from the average city life. This area of the California coast is known for it's moderate climate, wildlife, beautiful beaches and wharfs, and surf culture. This coastal gem also features many great local restaurants and shops, as well as a vibrant arts and music scene, among many other activities. It is a great place to explore the natural beauty of California while also participating in the laid back beach culture that Santa Cruz is known for.
